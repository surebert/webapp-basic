Beginning A Surebert Framework

Welcome to a new surebert framework. If you have questions you can email paul.visco AT roswellpark.org

  1. In order to get rid of this page, you need to go ahead and edit /public/.htaccess and comment change 

    RewriteRule .* index.html
    to 
    #RewriteRule .* index.html
    TIP: You can use this index.html as a static placeholder in an emergency, say if your DB is down. You may want to keep it around and remember what to uncomment to turn it back on!

  2. If you do not already have a copy, you will need to download the framework from surebert.com and place it somewhere on your server, e.g. /var/www/html/framework 

    svn export http://surebert.com/svn/sbf/trunk /var/www/html/sbf/2_5

  3. Then set your SUREBERT_FRAMEWORK_PATH in /private/config/definitions.php to where you have the framework stored 

    define("SUREBERT_FRAMEWORK_PATH", "/var/www/html/sbf/2_5");

  4. Visit your site, you should be ready to go! Read up on the surebert framework and toolkit and surebert.com and on the surebert wiki
